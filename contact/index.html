Contact Us

District 48 AA can be contacted using the following options:

  • Email: webchair@district48aa.org
  • Meeting: Second Wednesday of every month @ 7:00PM
    at Montoursville Step Group
  • Mail:
    PO Box 303
    Williamsport, PA 17701

We accept 7th-Tradition Contributions using Paypal.

Please note: Although we can try to help you locate a meeting in an area you may be visiting soon, you will be served much faster and more effectively if you search our meeting list.
